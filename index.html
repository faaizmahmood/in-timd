Meet the Team

Andy DaCosta
Co-founder & CEO

With 20 years in visual effects, Andy DaCosta has an impressive background working on feature films, television, and music videos. He has led film and commercial teams with his deep expertise, achieving significant milestones such as creating a fully computer-generated furniture line for Anthropologie and developing pipelines for several film and animation studios. His extensive credits include over 49 feature films, including "The Golden Compass," "Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man's Chest" and "At World's End," and "X-Men: First Class," earning 5 Oscar nominations, 4 BAFTA nominations, and 1 BAFTA win. InTimid was inspired by Andy’s personal experiences and challenges with modern dating culture, driving him to revolutionize online dating.

Chris Reid
Co-founder & CTO

Chris has over 20 years of software development experience and a passion for AI and machine learning. Pushing the boundaries of these technologies has led him to undertake a wide range of innovative projects- from seamlessly integrating AI into Hollywood applications to developing cutting-edge ML-powered software for the Department of Defense. At InTimid, he leverages AI and ML for precise matchmaking, combining technical expertise with a fascination for relationships. Previously, he held leadership roles at Hollywood VFX studios, developing tools that blend VFX and AI. Chris is also dedicated to inspiring the next generation to explore and embrace technology. Chris sees AI not as a replacement for human ingenuity, but as a powerful tool to enhance and augment our abilities.

Tiff Hicks
Entrepreneur

Tiffany is a Stanford graduate and second-generation entrepreneur whose passion for sales is built into her DNA. (Her father was one of the first black hires at IBM and Xerox, and later built a successful marketing business.) She cut her teeth at Yelp as an early sales hire and manager during their pre- and post-IPO phase, refined her complex selling skills at Salesforce and has spent the past decade of her career building revenue engines at fast-growth startups.

Cailin LaVallee
Advisor, Marketing Lead

Cailin is a seasoned marketer with a two-decade track record of propelling innovative businesses forward. She honed her craft at top-tier creative agencies, spearheading go-to-market strategies, crafting compelling messaging, and developing impactful content for tech brands of all sizes. At Google, her nine-year tenure took her from building Waze's B2B marketing team to leading marketing efforts for the Google Ads SMB segment, where she partnered with sales to drive triple-digit growth for emerging lines of business.
